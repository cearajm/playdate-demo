

Controls:
- press A to start the game.
- control pancake the hamster with the d-pad.
- press B to shoot sunflower seeds at the approaching enemy stoats.
- there is a 3 seed capacity. when pancake is out of seeds, he can't move!
- refill the seeds by spinning the crank 4 times. seeds can only be replenished when fully depleted.
- gain a point for each stoat defeated. the game ends when pancake dies, or a stoat gets away.
